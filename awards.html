Special Recognition

Honoring excellence in youth leadership and inspirational community service.

Pratibha Samman – Youth Day 2023

This honor was presented by Swami Vivekanand Seva Sansthan on the occasion of National Youth Day 2023, recognizing outstanding contributions to social service and leadership, inspired by Swami Vivekananda's powerful message: "Arise, awake, and stop not until the goal is reached."

Gratitude Award – 2023

Presented by Udra Charaitable Trust as a token of appreciation for spreading happiness and uplifting the spirit of the community with compassion and kindness.

Special Guest Honor – 2024

Awarded during the Mega Blood Donation Camp by Ocean of Faith Foundation, Jamshedpur, for supporting and encouraging voluntary blood donation and saving lives.

Foundation Day Honor – 2025

Awarded by Kshatriya Samaj Sampoorna Bharat on its Foundation Day 2025 for proudly upholding the values of bravery, pride, and social unity. Based on the motto: "Even if the head is sacrificed, pride should never bow."

Contribution to Society – 2023

Presented by Lakmé Academy Jamshedpur in recognition of our dedicated efforts, hard work, and meaningful contribution to the betterment of society.

Jamshedpur Pride Award – 2022

Awarded by Har Har Mahadev Seva Sangh during the 21st Bhajan Sandhya Ceremony for excellent service in the field of public welfare and social work.
