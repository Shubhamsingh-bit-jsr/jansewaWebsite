Our Journey

Established on October 2, 2016, and officially registered in 2021 (Registration Number: 2021/JSR/24/BK4/1), Jan Seva Sangh Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to the rescue, treatment, and care of cows and dogs. Our mission is to give a new life and a dignified existence to helpless and injured animals.

This service began with a heartfelt incident experienced by our founder, Mr. Suraj Kumar. One day, he saw a bull near his shop that had a deep injury in its leg and was unable to stand. He immediately called a doctor and arranged for its treatment. Miraculously, just 15 minutes after the treatment, the bull stood up again. This moment deeply touched him and made him realize that there must be many such animals in pain across the city with no one to care for them.

With this thought, Jan Seva Sangh Trust was born. He started this noble work along with a few friends, and today it has grown into a registered trust that continuously helps injured and stray animals.
